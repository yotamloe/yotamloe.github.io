About Me

With a passion for technology and a commitment to excellence, I specialize in crafting tailored software solutions that enhance system performance and reliability. My expertise lies in observability, microservices architecture, and Kubernetes orchestration.

  • Observability Expert: Implementing robust observability frameworks using tools like OpenTelemetry, Prometheus, and Grafana to provide actionable insights.
  • Microservices Architect: Designing and developing scalable microservices that align with your business objectives.
  • Kubernetes Specialist: Deploying and managing containerized applications for optimal resource utilization and uptime.

Leveraging programming languages such as Go, Python, and Node.js, I deliver solutions that are both efficient and maintainable. My goal is to empower businesses with the tools and knowledge they need to achieve full system visibility and operational excellence.

I am also an active contributor to the open-source community, enhancing projects that many organizations rely on daily. Explore my work on GitHub at github.com/yotamloe.
