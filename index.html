About Me

I am a passionate software developer with extensive experience in observability, microservice development, and Kubernetes. With a strong command of programming languages like Go, Python, and Node.js, I help businesses build scalable, reliable, and efficient software solutions.

  • Expert in designing and implementing microservices architectures.
  • Proficient in setting up observability frameworks using OpenTelemetry, Prometheus, and Grafana.
  • Experienced in deploying and managing applications on Kubernetes and Docker.

As an active member of the observability open-source community, I contribute to projects like OpenTelemetry, Prometheus, and OpenSearch. My work enables engineers to efficiently monitor their production environments and gain valuable insights from their data.

You can find some of my recent work and contributions on my GitHub profile at github.com/yotamloe.
